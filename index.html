About

Welcome to Who Wants Rubbish, a community platform dedicated to promoting sustainability by connecting businesses with individuals who can donate their unwanted items. Our mission is to reduce waste by providing businesses with an easy way to connect with members of their local community who are willing to donate their unwanted items. By doing so, we hope to reduce the amount of waste that ends up in landfills and promote a more sustainable way of living.

We believe that everyone can make a difference, no matter how small. By donating items that would otherwise go to waste, you are taking a step towards a more sustainable future.

Our community is made up of businesses and individuals who are passionate about reducing waste and promoting sustainability. We strive to make the process of donating and receiving items as easy and convenient as possible. If you're a business looking to donate your unwanted items, simply register on our platform and list the items you would like to donate. Users can then browse the listings and contact you to arrange pick up. If you're an individual looking to donate items, simply browse the listings and contact the businesses that are requesting the items you have.

Purpose

Purpose

Our website was founded with a simple yet powerful goal in mind: to reduce waste consumption and promote sustainability. The idea came to us after visiting a local tip and realizing just how much waste could be repurposed or repaired. With this website, we aim to connect businesses and charities, and encourage individuals to take action towards a more sustainable future

Environment

Environment

The impact of pollution and landfill on our environment is staggering. Every year, landfills across the globe are filled with millions of tons of waste that could be repurposed or recycled. Our website aims to help reduce this waste by connecting businesses and charities, and encouraging individuals to donate excess or unwanted inventory

Charities

Charities

Charities and shelters often struggle to find the resources they need to carry out their important work. With our website, charities can create listings for items they need, and businesses and individuals can donate excess or unwanted inventory. This helps to reduce waste and ensure that valuable resources are put to good use

Start-ups

Start-ups

Starting a new business can be tough, especially when it comes to sourcing materials and inventory. Our website provides a platform for entrepreneurs to create listings for items they need to get their business off the ground. By connecting with businesses and individuals who have excess inventory, start-ups can save money and reduce waste.

Action
